## Consent Script

Explain the study purpose, voluntary participation, right to withdraw, recording, anonymization, data storage, and any AI-assisted processing if applicable. Ask for explicit consent before recording.

## Warm-Up

1. Please briefly describe your role and experience related to this topic.
2. Can you describe a recent project or situation that connects to the study topic?

## Core Questions

| RQ | Main question | Follow-up probes |
|---|---|---|
| RQ1 | How do you understand the role of traditional craft in contemporary design? | Can you give an example? Has this changed over time? |
| RQ2 | What tensions appear when traditional techniques are used in current design practice? | Who is involved? What constraints matter most? |
| RQ3 | How do sustainability concerns affect design choices involving craft? | Environmental, cultural, educational, or market factors? |

## Closing

1. Is there anything important that I did not ask?
2. May I contact you later for clarification?
