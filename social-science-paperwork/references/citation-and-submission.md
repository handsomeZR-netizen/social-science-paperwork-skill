# Citation And Submission Checks

## Citation Integrity

For every claim with a citation, verify:

- The cited work exists.
- The metadata are correct.
- The cited page, section, or abstract supports the exact claim.
- The source is appropriate for the field.
- The citation is not being used for a broader claim than it supports.

Use `scripts/audit_references.py` to compare manuscript citations with BibTeX keys.

## Style Routing

Use:

- **APA 7** for psychology, education, communication, management, and many international social science journals.
- **GB/T 7714** for many Chinese journals and thesis requirements.
- **Journal-specific style** when the target journal gives explicit instructions.

Do not mix citation systems in one manuscript.

## Reporting Standards

Use the relevant checklist:

- APA JARS for quantitative, qualitative, and mixed methods.
- COREQ for interviews and focus groups.
- STROBE for observational studies.
- PRISMA or PRISMA-ScR for systematic or scoping reviews.

## Submission Audit

Before submission, check:

- Title, abstract, keywords, and research questions match.
- Method section matches actual procedure.
- Sample claims match sampling method.
- Ethics approval or exemption is stated where needed.
- Consent and anonymization are described for human subjects.
- Tables and figures are numbered, cited, and interpretable.
- Appendices include instruments if journal permits.
- AI use disclosure follows the journal policy.
- Data availability statement is accurate.
- References are complete and cited in text.

Use `assets/submission_checklist_template.md` as a working checklist.
